Solution

LEGO, but for Furniture.

Modular, Simple Furniture


We have all been through the pains of moving into a new home or apartment. Many college students endure this on a yearly basis, needing to sort out their keepsakes and constantly stay on the move. What if there was a more convenient way for people to easily and conveniently move from one place to another?
We are attempting to solve this problem by creating a singular, modular piece of furniture that can be configured into the essential furniture pieces and easily disassembled. Inspired by Lego and building blocks, we have simplified furniture pieces down to be able to be functional as different pieces of furniture when built in certain ways. We hope to get this to retail stores around the nation through our technical, design, and managerial skills.

Many Configurations, 3 Parts


Create the dream furniture piece with just 3 of our parts. Using a top, screw, and leg, you have unlimited choices to create a furniture piece in parts you can easily assemble and disassemble at need.
